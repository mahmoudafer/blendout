The Original Blendout

We created a small power-machine that lets you make fresh smoothies, shakes, and baby food wherever you are. Just add the ingredients, press the button, and your powerful Blender will make your favorite recipes.

Blendout is small in size, portable, and has multiple uses, and can be used as a cup besides a blender. It can mix all kinds of fruits and vegetables.

We take safety seriously, that's why Blendout has both a safety protection switch and a charge/discharge protection. Besides, Blendout is environmentally friendly and healthy as we use new and nontoxic PP/PC material.

Blendout has a powerful battery-operated blender that can produce 10-12 cups per charge. Simply charge via USB port for 1-3 hours before initial use, then add your ingredients and power-on for a tasty smoothie shake in 90 seconds!

Features

01. Blend All Fruits

Enjoy making a FRESH cocktail of all your favorite fruits and vegetables anytime anywhere in the world!

02. Ice CRUSHER

Your Blendount can blend ice, allowing you to make cold smoothies and getting you ready for the hot weather

03. Self Cleaner

After finishing your fresh smoothie, put a bit of soap with water in your Blendout and press the button and Voila! your Blendout is clean and ready for another adventure.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where are you based? Where do you ship from?

We are based in Lebanon. All Blendout orders ship directly from our office in Saida City.

How long will it take for my order to arrive?

We deliver to anywhere in Lebanon within 3 working days

How do I clean my Blendout blender?

We recommend after each use to blend water with a drop of soap to clean your Blendout. Hold on the button with plain water, and you're ready to go again.

How much does my Blendout weigh?

Your Blendout weighs 1lb, so it's light enough to toss in your bag and go!

Can I fly with my Blendout?

Yes! We recommend that you bring your Blendout in your checked bag without any liquid inside.

Does it have Filter?

Yes, our Blendout also features a built-in strainer to help keep your beverages chunk free.
